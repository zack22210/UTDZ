## Optimizing Your Zeni Spending for Maximum Impact

Earning Zeni is only half the battle; spending it wisely is equally critical. Haphazard spending can leave you short when you need it most. Prioritizing your Zeni expenditures will ensure your resources contribute most effectively to your overall power.

### Prioritize Unit Upgrades

Your units are your primary defense, so investing Zeni into their power is almost always a good decision. Focus on your core team first.

*   **Core Team Focus:** Identify your strongest and most versatile units and pour your Zeni into upgrading them. A few maxed-out units are often more effective than many partially upgraded ones.
*   **Evolution Costs:** Save Zeni for unit evolutions, which typically provide significant power spikes but come with a higher Zeni cost.

### Unlock Essential Base Enhancements

Some games feature base upgrades that provide passive bonuses or unlock new functionalities. These can be valuable Zeni sinks that offer long-term benefits.

*   **Increased Storage:** More inventory space means less need to sell valuable items prematurely.
*   **Passive Income Generators:** If available, structures that generate Zeni over time are excellent investments.

*   **Temporary Buffs:** Purchase buffs for difficult stages if you're struggling, but don't rely on them constantly.
*   **Missing Evolution Materials:** Sometimes, a small Zeni purchase can complete an evolution, unlocking a powerful unit immediately.
