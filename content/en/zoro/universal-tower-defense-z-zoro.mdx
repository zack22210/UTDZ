## The Path to Power: Evolution and Etherealize Progression

Unlocking the full potential of your units in Universal Tower Defense Z Zoro requires more than just leveling; it demands engaging with the deep progression systems of Etherealization and Evolution. These processes transform your basic summons into formidable forces capable of conquering high-wave Infinite runs and Ultra Bosses.

Here's a step-by-step guide to efficiently evolve your units into endgame powerhouses:

1.  **Select a Core Carry:** Identify a high-rarity unit (e.g., Triple Threat or King Sailor) that you intend to keep in your active roster long-term. Avoid wasting precious materials on temporary starter units that will soon be replaced.
2.  **Max Out Base Levels:** Farm Story Mode or replayable Virtual Realm stages to maximize your chosen unit's base level. This step is vital for understanding their attack patterns and range before investing premium resources.
3.  **Etherealize for Power Spikes:** Utilize Etherealization Shards on your selected unit. Etherealizing provides a significant stat multiplier, making your unit considerably stronger and more capable of handling tougher enemy waves and bosses.
4.  **Gather Evolution Materials:** Collect specific evolution items, fragments, and catalysts. These are primarily obtained from Raids and Event Modes. Each major unit typically requires a unique set of materials for Evolution.
5.  **Execute the Evolution:** Once all materials are gathered, evolve your unit. This process unlocks a completely new form, featuring enhanced skills, wider attack areas (AOE), and superior base scaling. Evolved units retain their levels but gain access to vastly improved skill trees and trait compatibility. Always prioritize evolving your primary damage dealer first to maximize your offensive capabilities.
