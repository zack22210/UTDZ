## Core Components of a Universal Tower Defense Z Meta Optimizer

Units are the backbone of any tower defense strategy. A meta optimizer helps you identify the strongest damage dealers, support units, and economy generators. Traits offer significant customization, allowing you to tailor units to specific roles or enhance their innate strengths. The right trait can turn a good unit into an exceptional one. However, rerolling for perfect traits can be resource-intensive, making an optimizer's guidance invaluable.

A meta optimizer will guide you on:
*   **Optimal Traits per Unit:** Which traits best complement a unit's role (e.g., damage traits for attackers, economy traits for money generators).

*   **Pity Systems:** Awareness of systems like Astral 277 or Fortunate 125 pity helps in planning your rerolls.

### Relics: Gear Up for Victory

Relics function as equipment, providing stat bonuses and unique effects that further amplify your units' capabilities. Just like traits, selecting and upgrading the right relics is crucial for maximizing performance.

An optimizer helps with:
*   **Best-in-Slot Recommendations:** Identifying which relics are ideal for specific units or team compositions.

*   **Relic Score Analysis:** Tools that grade your relics (D to SSS tier) can help you assess their quality and prioritize upgrades.
